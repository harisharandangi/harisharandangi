## About Me

I am a **Mechanical and Mechatronics Engineer** currently pursuing an **M.Sc. in Mechatronics and Cyber-Physical Systems** at the **Deggendorf Institute of Technology, Germany**.

My work focuses on the intersection of:

- Robotics and autonomous systems  
- Control systems, LQR, PID, and state-space modeling  
- SLAM-based navigation and mobile robot simulation  
- CAD/CAE, FEM, CFD, and mechanical simulation  
- Additive manufacturing and digital manufacturing workflows  
- Engineering software platforms for CAD and vehicle systems  

I am also the **Founder & Managing Director of GadiDokan Pvt. Ltd.**, where I apply mechanical engineering, repair operations, procurement, and digital systems to vehicle-focused services.
